About Marine Consultants

Marine Consultants Limited is built on a legacy of nautical mastery. Our logo, the sextant, characterizes our philosophy: an eminently practical tool that ensures safe navigation through any marketplace.

Based in the Port of Spain and Chaguaramas area of Trinidad, we draw on a network of local and international expertise to provide world-class products and services to sectors including Oil and Gas, Marine, National Security, and Pleasure Crafts.
