# Audit: Reference vs. Current Implementation ## 1. Product Category Depth & Subcategories **Reference (mcl.co.tt):** - **Chains, Ropes & Rigging:** Includes Mooring Systems, Lifting Equipment, Wire Ropes, and related hardware. - **Charts, Publications & Instruments:** Deep technical inventory including Admiralty Charts, nautical publications, and precision navigational instruments (sextants, etc.). - **Engines:** Major focus on marine propulsion, power generation, and spare parts. - **Fire Fighting:** Specialized equipment, extinguishers, and suppression systems. - **Lifeboats & Davits:** SURVITEC and Viking products, davit-launched liferafts, and roll-off cradles. **Current (Your Site):** - Top-level categories exist but lack the *nested sub-category structure* found on the reference. - Individual product detail pages are missing for technical items like "Viking Braidline" or "Admiralty Standard Charts". ## 2. Service Pages & Technical Coverage **Reference:** - Strong focus on **Inspection & Certification** (ISO 9001:2015, STOW). - Detailed service lines: NDT (Non-Destructive Testing), Mechanical Maintenance, and Asset Management. **Current:** - Service pages are high-level but miss the *specific regional support protocols* and *certified service capacity* metrics mentioned in the reference. ## 3. Request Quote Flow & CTAs **Reference:** - Quote requests are contextually placed within product categories. - Immediate "Contact Us" or "Inquire" options on almost every technical landing point. **Current:** - Quote flow is a standalone page. It needs better *on-page integration* within the product browsing experience (e.g., "Inquire about this category"). ## 4. About & Company Detail **Reference:** - Heavy emphasis on "Founded in 1971" and "Master Mariner" leadership. - Geographic specificity: Trinidad (Port of Spain/Chaguaramas). **Current:** - Company info is improved but can be further refined with more *local regional detail* and specific historical milestones. ## 5. Contact & Internal Linking **Reference:** - Map links and specific regional hub contacts (Trinidad focus). - Deep internal linking between Services and Products (e.g., maintenance service linking to the engine parts catalog). **Current:** - Linking is broad; it needs to be more *technically specific* (e.g., the Maintenance service page should link directly to the Spare Parts product subcategory).
