Regional Procurement FAQ

Practical information for regional equipment acquisition and support.

What is the typical lead time for regional inventory?

In-stock regional inventory can be dispatched within 48 hours to major ports in Trinidad. Specialized rigging or electronics may require 7-14 business days for technical configuration.

Are all products compliant with local maritime standards?

Yes. Our inventory is specifically curated to meet the regulatory requirements of the Trinidad & Tobago Maritime Services Division and international SOLAS standards.

Do you provide on-site installation for navigation aids?

Marine Consultants offers a dedicated technical team for the installation and calibration of navigation aids and bridge electronics across regional coastal sites.

Can custom chain and rigging lengths be ordered?

Absolutely. We provide custom cutting, splicing, and load testing services at our regional facility to meet specific mooring and lifting requirements.
