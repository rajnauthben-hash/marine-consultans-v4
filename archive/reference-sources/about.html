The 1971 Foundation

Marine Consultants Limited was founded in Trinidad & Tobago in 1971 by a visionary group of Master Mariners. Their goal was to establish a locally-rooted yet internationally-standardized technical authority for the region's expanding industrial shipping lanes.

For over half a century, we have remained independent and steadfast, providing the technical precision and command-level expertise required for complex maritime operations in the southern Caribbean and beyond.
